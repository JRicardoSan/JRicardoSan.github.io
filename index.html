J. Ricardo Sánchez Ibáñez

Research Assistant

Space Robotics Laboratory at University of Málaga

Biography

J. Ricardo is a PhD student from the University of Malaga. His research is focused on developing path planning solutions for mobile robots in unstructured scenarios. He has experience working with experimental rovers, having carried out several stays at ESA-ESTEC Planetary Robotics Laboratory in Noordwijk, the Netherlands. Currently, Ricardo is working under the H2020 project ADE (OG10). He is developing path and motion planning techniques to control a mobile manipulator, which has to perform autonomous approach-and-sample operations as part of an hypothetical planetary exploration mission.
